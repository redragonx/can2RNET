Control layout for my particular controller (may vary for others).

Layout with LED on (center bottom button to toggle):
axes 0/1: left stick
axes 2/3: right stick
axes 4/5: DPAD
btn 0-3: left/up/down/right
btn 4-5: left trigger top/bottom
btn 6-7: right trigger top/bottom
btn 8-9: center button left/right
btn 10-11: press left/right stick
